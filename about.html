About NEX

Building the next generation of diplomats, speakers, changemakers, and visionary leaders.

Our Mission

At NEX, we believe debate is more than competition — it is a transformative platform that empowers students to think critically, communicate fearlessly, and lead meaningfully. Our mission is to cultivate a community where young minds can express ideas openly, engage in global conversations, and develop the confidence to become future leaders.

A Note From The Founder

Hi! This is Hasinimalar Ganeshkumar — The Founder of NEX. NEX was founded with the vision of creating a platform where students can discover the power of communication, diplomacy, and leadership. We aim to build an inspiring environment where every student feels heard, valued, and empowered to create meaningful impact through ideas and discussion.
